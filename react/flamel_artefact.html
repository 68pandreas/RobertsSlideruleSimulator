Nicolas Flamel's artefact

Born in 1340, Nicolas Flamel is a well known alchemist. He discovered "Philosopher's Stone", a substance capable of turning base metals into gold. He also created "Elixir of Life", which grants the drinker eternal life. Currently he lives with his wife, Pernelle, in Paris.

It is little know that Nicolas Flamel used in his research a mysterius artefact, similar to the one used by Hiram Abif. It made it possible to calculate molecular masses of chemical elements and compounds in seconds rather than finding them through research.
