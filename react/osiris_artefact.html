Osiris artefact

Before you are allowed to access Hiram Abif's artefact your principles need to be put to the test. As the artefact you are seeking is very powerful, these tests are, in fact, for your safety and protection!

You will begin your tests on a much smaller artefact, which was used by Egyptian Priests for calculating the positions of the planets as well as for construction work.

The egyptian artefact consists of two rules, which are immovable, and one movable slide. There is a also a movable cursor window with hairline. It is constructed from wood and straw.

You will learn how to slide the cursor and/or slide by dragging them with your mouse. You will also learn how to move them automatically by right-clicking on scales and entering numerical values. It is also possible to read the numerical position of hairline by checking the "Markings" checkbox.
