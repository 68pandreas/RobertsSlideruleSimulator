Collection of Slide Rule replicas

Slide rules are not manufactured any more.
The ones, which still exist, will eventually deteriorate with time.

Here you will find a collection of some of the most prominent models carefully hand-crafted and reproduced in JavaScript.
They can be yours right now, totally free.
All you need to do is to Save Page As... in your browser and the slide rule replica is yours forever.

(Technical information: all replicas can work without any active internet connection and as such, they are digitally safe.)

Quick manual
  • Drag rule or cursor with mouse.
  • Use mouse wheel to zoom-in or zoom-out.
  • Click on checkboxes for appropriate actions.
  • Right-click on cursor or on scale to enter digital values for automation. You can:
    • Enter integer or floating-point value, i.e.: 3.14
    • Enter degrees using ":", i.e.: 2:30:26
    • Enter pre-defined constants: pi, pi1, e, e1, c, c1, q
    • To navigate to additional hairlines on the cursor, press and hold CTRL key while right-clicking.
      (You will also need to right-click on the appropriate side of the cursor.)
  • You can download the entire collection to your computer and run locally (without internet connection).
  • Once you download the entire collection you can start building your own models. Simply, open one of the models in text editor, see how it is constructed and enjoy coding.
    (Yes, you will need some JavaScript and HTML skills.)
