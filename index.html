Collection of Slide Rule replicas

Slide rules are not manufactured any more.
The ones, which still exist, will eventually deteriorate with time.

On this site you will find a collection of some of the most prominent models
carefully hand-crafted and reproduced in JavaScript.
And most importantly, they can be yours right now, totally free.
All you need to do is to Save Page As... in your browser of choice
and the slide rule replica is yours forever.

(Technical information: all replicas can work without any active internet connection. They do not rely on any external digital communication and as such, are digitally safe.)
