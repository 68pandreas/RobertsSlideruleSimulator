The slide rules above are very close replicas of existing models.
  • Drag rule or cursor with mouse.
  • Use mouse wheel to zoom-in or zoom-out.
  • Click on checkboxes for appropriate actions.
  • Right-click on cursor or on scale to enter digital values. You can:
    • Enter integer or floating-point value, i.e.: 3.14
    • Enter degrees using ":", i.e.: 2:30:26
    • Enter pre-defined constants: pi, pi1, e, e1, c, c1, q
  • You can download the entire collection to your computer and run locally (without internet connection).
  • Once you download the entire collection you can start building your own models. Simply, open one of the models in text editor, see how it works and enjoy coding.
